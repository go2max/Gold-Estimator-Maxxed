# Safe Reference Sources

Reviewed June 21, 2026.

- OSHA, Crystalline Silica: https://www.osha.gov/silica-crystalline
- CDC/NIOSH, Mining safety and health: https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/mining/

The in-app reference is deliberately limited to dust precautions, personal protective equipment, mechanical classification, water-based gravity methods, specimen preservation, and following equipment and local land/water rules. It does not provide chemical recovery or home furnace procedures.
